Accommodation

Off-campus:

Recommended Options

You are recommended to stay in Holiday Inn Express Hong Kong Kowloon East or Crowne Plaza Hong Kong Kowloon East during your visit. It is located in Tseung Kwan O with about 10-minute taxi ride from HKUST, and a 45-minute drive from the Hong Kong International Airport. Booking can be made directly to the hotels.
